Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any setup or implementation fees?

Implementation fees vary based on property size and complexity. Essential plans typically have a one-time setup fee of $1,500, while Professional and Enterprise plans include custom implementation services with fees determined during consultation.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time with the changes taking effect immediately. Downgrades can be processed at the end of your current billing cycle.

What kind of support is included?

Essential plans include email support with 24-hour response times. Professional plans include 24/7 priority support via email and phone. Enterprise plans include a dedicated account manager and technical support team.

Do you offer custom development for specific needs?

Yes, custom development services are available for Enterprise plans. Our team can build custom features and integrations specific to your property's unique requirements.
