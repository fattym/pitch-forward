Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our connectivity solutions.

Dedicated internet provides a fixed bandwidth exclusively for your business, ensuring consistent performance regardless of other users. Shared internet connections divide bandwidth among multiple users, which can result in variable speeds during peak usage times. Our dedicated fiber solutions offer guaranteed bandwidth with symmetrical upload and download speeds, making them ideal for businesses with critical connectivity needs.

Our 99.99% uptime guarantee is backed by our Service Level Agreement (SLA). This means your connectivity will be available 99.99% of the time, which translates to less than 52.6 minutes of downtime per year. If we fail to meet this guarantee, you'll receive service credits according to our SLA terms. We achieve this reliability through redundant network paths, automatic failover systems, and 24/7 proactive monitoring of our infrastructure.

Yes, our connectivity solutions are designed to scale with your business. In most cases, bandwidth upgrades can be implemented without hardware changes or service interruptions. Our fiber infrastructure supports speeds from 100 Mbps to 10 Gbps, allowing for seamless upgrades as your needs evolve. You can request bandwidth changes through your account manager or our customer portal, and most upgrades can be completed within 24-48 hours.

Our connectivity solutions include various security features depending on your selected plan. Basic plans include standard firewall protection and DDoS mitigation. Advanced plans add intrusion prevention systems (IPS), web filtering, and application control. Enterprise plans include comprehensive security with advanced threat protection, SIEM integration, and optional managed security services. All plans can be enhanced with our VPN services for secure remote access and site-to-site connectivity.

Installation timeframes vary based on your location and the type of connectivity solution. For locations where our fiber infrastructure is already present, installation typically takes 10-15 business days. For new fiber builds, the timeframe can range from 30-90 days depending on construction requirements and permits. Wireless solutions can often be deployed more quickly, typically within 5-10 business days. During the sales process, we'll provide you with a specific installation timeline for your location.
