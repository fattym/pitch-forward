Fuel Level Sensors

  • High-precision capacitive sensors with ±1% accuracy
  • Real-time measurement frequency: Every 30 seconds
  • Temperature-compensated readings (-40°C to +85°C)

Flow Meters

  • Digital flow meters with 0.5% measurement accuracy
