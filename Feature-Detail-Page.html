Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our real-time vehicle tracking solution.

Our GPS tracking system provides accuracy within 2-3 meters under optimal conditions. The system uses multi-satellite connectivity (GPS, GLONASS, and Galileo) to ensure the highest possible accuracy even in challenging environments such as urban canyons or areas with limited satellite visibility. For most fleet management applications, this level of accuracy is more than sufficient for identifying vehicle location, monitoring routes, and enabling efficient dispatching.

Our tracking solution requires a GPS tracking device to be installed in each vehicle. We offer several hardware options:

  • OBD-II Plug-and-Play Device: Simply plugs into the vehicle's OBD-II port for easy self-installation. Ideal for passenger vehicles and light-duty trucks.
  • Hardwired GPS Tracker: Professionally installed and connected directly to the vehicle's power supply. Recommended for heavy-duty vehicles or when a concealed installation is preferred.
  • Battery-Powered Asset Tracker: For non-powered assets or equipment that doesn't have a constant power source.

All hardware options are weatherproof, tamper-resistant, and designed for the demanding environment of vehicle operations.

Our standard configuration updates vehicle location every 30 seconds while the vehicle is in motion. This frequency can be customized based on your specific needs:

  • Standard Tracking: 30-second intervals (default)
  • High-Frequency Tracking: 5-10 second intervals for applications requiring more precise tracking
  • Power-Saving Mode: 2-5 minute intervals for extended battery life in battery-powered units

Additionally, the system records specific events regardless of the update interval, such as ignition on/off, harsh braking, rapid acceleration, and geofence entry/exit.

Yes, we offer a comprehensive mobile app for both iOS and Android devices. The mobile app provides:

  • Real-time vehicle location viewing on a map
  • Push notifications for alerts and events
  • Driver identification and status updates
  • Trip history and reporting
  • Geofence management
  • Two-way communication with drivers

The app is designed for both fleet managers and drivers, with different permission levels and user interfaces tailored to each role. All data is synchronized in real-time with the web-based dashboard.

We take data security extremely seriously. Our system implements multiple layers of security:

  • Data Encryption: All data is encrypted both in transit (256-bit SSL/TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Control: Role-based access control with multi-factor authentication options
  • Compliance: SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant
  • Data Centers: All data is stored in secure, redundant data centers with 99.9% uptime
  • Audit Logging: Comprehensive audit trails of all system access and changes

We also provide data retention policies that can be customized to meet your organization's specific requirements and compliance needs.

Yes, our system is designed with integration in mind. We offer several integration options:

  • REST API: Comprehensive API for bidirectional data exchange
  • Webhooks: Real-time event notifications pushed to your systems
  • Pre-built Connectors: Ready-to-use integrations with popular business systems including:
    • ERP systems (SAP, Oracle, Microsoft Dynamics)
    • CRM platforms (Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho)
    • Maintenance management systems
    • Accounting software
    • Fuel card systems
  • Custom Integration: Our professional services team can develop custom integrations for your specific needs

Integration capabilities vary by subscription plan, with more advanced options available in the Professional and Enterprise tiers.
