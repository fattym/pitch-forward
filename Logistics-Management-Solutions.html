Our fleet tracking solution utilizes compact GPS tracking devices that can be easily installed in any vehicle. These devices connect to our cloud platform via cellular networks and provide real-time location data, engine diagnostics, and driver behavior metrics. We offer both hardwired and plug-and-play options. For advanced functionality like temperature monitoring or cargo tracking, additional sensors can be integrated with the base tracking unit.

Our route optimization engine uses advanced algorithms that consider multiple variables including distance, traffic patterns, delivery time windows, vehicle capacity, driver schedules, and special handling requirements. The system can generate optimal routes for hundreds of stops in seconds, and can dynamically re-optimize routes as conditions change throughout the day. Dispatchers can manually adjust routes when needed, and drivers receive turn-by-turn navigation through our mobile app.
