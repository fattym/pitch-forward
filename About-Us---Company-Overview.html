Our Mission & Vision

Founded in 2005, TechSolutions has grown from a small IT consulting firm to a global technology leader with offices in 12 countries. Our mission is to empower businesses through technology, providing innovative solutions that drive efficiency, security, and growth.

We believe technology should work for people, not the other way around. That's why we focus on creating intuitive, powerful solutions that solve real business challenges and create meaningful impact.

Our Journey

From our humble beginnings to becoming a global technology leader, explore the key milestones that have shaped our company's growth and success.

2005

Company Founded

TechSolutions was founded by Michael Richardson and a small team of technology experts with a vision to transform how businesses leverage technology.

2010

International Expansion

Opened our first international offices in London and Singapore, beginning our global expansion to better serve clients worldwide.

2015

Cloud Solutions Launch

Launched our comprehensive suite of cloud solutions, helping businesses transition to more flexible and scalable IT infrastructure.

2018

AI & Machine Learning Division

Established our AI and Machine Learning division to develop cutting-edge solutions for data analysis, automation, and predictive analytics.

2022

Forbes Technology Leader Award

Recognized as a global technology leader by Forbes, highlighting our commitment to innovation and excellence in IT solutions.

2025

Sustainable Technology Initiative

Launched our Sustainable Technology Initiative, committed to developing eco-friendly solutions and achieving carbon neutrality by 2030.
