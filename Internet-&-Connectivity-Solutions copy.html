Fiber Optic Connectivity Overview

Our enterprise-grade fiber optic solutions deliver unmatched speed, reliability, and security for businesses that demand the highest performance from their network infrastructure. With dedicated bandwidth and symmetrical upload and download speeds, our fiber solutions ensure your critical applications perform optimally at all times.

Fiber optic technology uses light signals transmitted through glass fibers to deliver data at speeds far exceeding traditional copper-based connections. This technology provides inherent security advantages, greater bandwidth capacity, and superior resistance to electromagnetic interference and environmental factors.

Key Advantages

  • Symmetrical bandwidth up to 10 Gbps
  • Ultra-low latency (< 5ms)
  • 99.99% uptime guarantee with SLA
  • Dedicated, unshared connections
  • Scalable to grow with your business

Ideal For

  • Enterprise headquarters and data centers
  • Cloud service providers and SaaS companies
  • Healthcare facilities and telemedicine
  • Financial institutions and trading firms
  • Media production and broadcasting

Did You Know?

Fiber optic cables transmit data at approximately 70% the speed of light, allowing information to travel around the world in just 0.2 seconds. Our enterprise fiber solutions leverage this technology to provide the fastest and most reliable connectivity available today.

SLA Terms & Conditions

Measurement Period

SLA metrics are measured and calculated on a calendar month basis. Credits are applied to the following month's invoice.

Exclusions

The following events are excluded from SLA calculations:

  • Scheduled maintenance with at least 48 hours advance notice
  • Force majeure events (natural disasters, acts of war, etc.)
  • Issues with customer equipment or facilities
  • Customer-caused outages or disruptions
  • Emergency maintenance to prevent network-wide issues

Credit Request Process

Customers must request SLA credits within 30 days of the incident by submitting a ticket through the customer portal or contacting their account manager. Credits will be applied to the next billing cycle.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our fiber optic connectivity solutions.

Shared fiber connections divide bandwidth among multiple customers, similar to traditional cable or DSL services. This means your actual speeds can vary based on network congestion, especially during peak usage times.

Our dedicated fiber solutions provide a fixed bandwidth exclusively for your business, ensuring consistent performance 24/7 regardless of what other users are doing. With dedicated fiber, you receive the full bandwidth you pay for with symmetrical upload and download speeds, making it ideal for businesses with critical connectivity needs.

Installation timeframes vary depending on your location and existing infrastructure. For locations where our fiber network is already present, installation typically takes 2-3 weeks from contract signing to service activation.

For locations requiring new fiber construction, the process can take 30-90 days depending on permitting requirements, construction complexity, and local regulations. During your initial consultation, we'll provide a specific timeline based on your location and requirements.

Our network is designed with multiple redundancies to minimize the risk of outages. In the rare event of a service disruption, our 24/7 monitoring systems typically detect issues before they impact your business, allowing our team to begin resolution immediately.

If you experience a service issue, our technical support team is available 24/7/365 via phone, email, or our customer portal. Our SLAs guarantee specific response and resolution times based on your service tier, and we provide service credits for any downtime that exceeds our guaranteed uptime percentage.

Yes, our fiber solutions are designed to scale with your business. In most cases, bandwidth upgrades can be implemented remotely without requiring equipment changes or service interruptions.

Our fiber infrastructure supports speeds from 100 Mbps to 10 Gbps, allowing for seamless upgrades as your needs evolve. You can request bandwidth changes through your account manager or our customer portal, and most upgrades can be completed within 24-48 hours.

Fiber optic connections offer inherent security advantages over traditional copper-based connections, as they're extremely difficult to tap without detection. Beyond this physical security, our fiber solutions include:

  • Basic DDoS protection on all service tiers
  • Advanced DDoS mitigation options
  • Optional managed firewall services
  • Traffic encryption capabilities
  • 24/7 network monitoring for security threats
  • Compliance with industry standards (HIPAA, PCI DSS, etc.)

Our Enterprise and Ultra tiers include enhanced security features, and we offer additional security services that can be tailored to your specific requirements.

Yes, all our fiber solutions include comprehensive SLAs that guarantee specific performance metrics, including:
