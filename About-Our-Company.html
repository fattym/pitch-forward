Our Journey

From our humble beginnings to becoming an industry leader, our history reflects our commitment to innovation and excellence.

2005

Foundation

Founded by a team of technology enthusiasts with a vision to transform how businesses leverage technology. Started with a small office in Nairobi with just 5 employees focused on basic IT services.

2010

Expansion into Fleet Management

Launched our first comprehensive fleet management solution, revolutionizing how businesses track and manage their vehicle assets. Expanded to 25 employees and moved to a larger office space.

2015

Regional Growth

Expanded operations across East Africa with new offices in Tanzania and Uganda. Introduced innovative solutions for the hospitality and healthcare sectors, growing our client base to over 100 organizations.

2018

Technology Innovation Award

Recognized with the prestigious African Technology Innovation Award for our integrated IoT solutions. Reached a milestone of 500 clients and 150 employees across multiple countries.

2023

Global Partnerships

Formed strategic partnerships with global technology leaders to enhance our solution offerings. Launched our AI-powered analytics platform, setting new standards for data-driven decision making across industries.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower organizations across Africa with innovative technology solutions that drive efficiency, growth, and competitive advantage. We are committed to delivering excellence in every aspect of our service, ensuring our clients achieve their full potential.

We believe in creating lasting partnerships with our clients, understanding their unique challenges, and developing tailored solutions that address their specific needs.

Our Vision

To be the leading technology solutions provider in Africa, recognized for innovation, reliability, and transformative impact across industries. We envision a future where every business, regardless of size, has access to world-class technology solutions that drive success.

Our vision extends beyond business success to creating positive social impact through technology, contributing to economic growth and development across the continent.
